### 2.1 
> **Under the very-high-emissions scenario SSP 5 (RCP 8.5), 30% of all ensemble members project a decrease in the yearly total runoff of more than...**

a. ...18%...

b. ...27%...

> **...until the period 2041-2070,**
> 
> **while 50% of all ensemble members predict either a decrease or an increase of up to...**

c. ...8%.

d. ...13%

```{admonition} Here's the solution
:class: dropdown
a. 18%   and   c. 8%
```
